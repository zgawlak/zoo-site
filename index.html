ABOUT US

WELCOME TO
DURHAM ZOO

Founded with a vision to inspire environmental consciousness and a passion for preserving Earth's treasures, Durham Zoo has evolved into a sanctuary where both animals and visitors thrive. Spanning over acres of carefully designed exhibits, our zoo offers an immersive encounter with creatures that span from the depths of the rainforests to the heights of the savannahs.

Each enclosure is thoughtfully crafted to mirror the animals' native habitats, allowing them to exhibit their natural behaviors while granting guests a window into their lives. Beyond its role as a recreational destination, Durham Zoo takes pride in its role as an educational institution, fostering a sense of wonder and curiosity about the world's ecosystems and the delicate balance that sustains them. Join us on an expedition of discovery at Durham Zoo, where conservation meets captivating adventure.
